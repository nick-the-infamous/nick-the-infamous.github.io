My Build Philosophy

My approach to builds focuses on accessibility and effectiveness, particularly for newer or more casual players.

I focus on Viper's, Berserker's, and Assassin's gear because these sets are more flexible across different builds and classes. This makes them more cost-efficient as your first ascended sets, as they can be used effectively on multiple characters and builds. I avoid more specialized stat combinations even in cases where they may offer more damage (see grieving 1 button paragon, etc.) since they offer less flexibility and may not be as versatile for players building their initial sets.

I design builds to hit critical hit chance caps without relying on food buffs or infusions. This makes the builds more sustainable for regular play and raid training without requiring players to spend gold on expensive consumables just to make the build function.
