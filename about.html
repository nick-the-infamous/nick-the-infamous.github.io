My Background

I'm a veteran Guild Wars 2 player who has been playing on and off since the game's original release. Over time I've accumulated over 2,000 raid boss kills and most (but not all) CM clears. I haven't been playing in the last few years but I came back recently and it's been a lot of fun.

I also don't claim to be a pro player. The stuff I create may be further optimized so I don't claim that I have the absolute best stuff or best knowledge of the game.
