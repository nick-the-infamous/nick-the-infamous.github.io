Welcome to LI Builds ▸
Expectations ▸

LI builds are designed around comfort and ease of use, with varying performance based on the number of active skills required. Below you'll find two ways to understand what to expect from these builds:

Number of Buttons

1-2 Button Builds

These builds focus on maximum accessibility with minimal inputs. Perfect for beginners or those who prefer minimal complexity. They're ideal for learning encounters with minimal mental overhead while still contributing meaningfully.

3-4 Button Builds

These builds strike a balance between performance and accessibility, requiring slightly more engagement. They introduce basic skill prioritization while remaining approachable for players comfortable with their class fundamentals.

5+ Button Builds

These builds offer higher performance at the cost of some accessibility. They require understanding of skill priorities and basic rotation concepts, making them ideal for players who want strong performance while maintaining a manageable learning curve.

DPS Performance

30-32k DPS

These builds typically place you in the lower third of DPS charts in most public groups.

33-35k DPS

In public groups, you can expect to place in the middle of DPS charts with these builds.

36-38k+ DPS

These higher performing builds will place you in the upper third of DPS in most public groups.

Real Fights

While the builds have some trade-offs (outlined below), don't let that discourage you! These builds are more than capable of handling most content in the game and will serve you well. I just want to call out some obvious downsides.

Power Builds

These builds may show lower damage in burst-oriented fights due to their consistent, sustained damage output. While standard power builds typically have strong burst openers (strong initial DPS burst in the first 10 to 20 seconds before tapering off), these LI versions may start slower but will often catch up as the fight progresses.

Condition Builds

Some of these condition builds require A LOT of time to ramp up their damage, which can result in lower performance in phased encounters. Additionally, torment-heavy builds may show reduced effectiveness against highly mobile bosses, as movement reduces torment's damage output.

Note: The relationship between button count and DPS output can vary by class and build. Some classes may achieve higher DPS with fewer buttons due to their design and available tools.

Important Practice Tips

To get accurate benchmark results, ensure you have:

  • Correct stats, gear pieces, runes, and sigils equipped
  • All traits matching the build exactly
  • Appropriate food and utility buffs (10% damage increase)
  • Proper infusions (optional, maybe 1% at most damage increase)
  • Relevant boons applied to yourself depending on build
  • Appropriate conditions on the golem depending on build
